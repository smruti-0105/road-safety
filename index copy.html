SAFETY STATISTICS

1.3 million people die in road crashes each year globally.

20-50 million people suffer non-fatal injuries annually.

90% of road accidents are caused by human error.

Safe Driving Tips

Always wear your seatbelt.

Don’t use your phone while driving.

Follow speed limits and traffic rules.

Avoid driving under the influence.

Stay focused and avoid distractions.
